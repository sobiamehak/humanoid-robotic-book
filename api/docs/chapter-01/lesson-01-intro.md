# Introduction to Humanoid Robotics

## What is a Humanoid Robot?

A humanoid robot is a robot with physical characteristics that resemble the human body. These robots typically have a head, torso, two arms, and two legs, though some may have additional features.

## History and Evolution

The concept of humanoid robots has fascinated humans for centuries. From ancient myths to modern robotics, the pursuit of creating human-like machines has driven innovation in multiple fields.

## Applications

Humanoid robots have various applications including:

- Research and development
- Educational tools
- Service robots
- Entertainment
- Healthcare assistance

## Key Challenges

Creating effective humanoid robots involves solving complex challenges in:

- Balance and locomotion
- Manipulation and dexterity
- Human-robot interaction
- Artificial intelligence
